# THE VEILWRACK — BESTIARY
Mythras Imperative stat blocks. Each is loaded as a `myth-creature-template`
by the seed script; use `spawn` to instantiate combat-ready NPCs.

## Hushed Alar
*An Alar emptied by a Stilling. Feathers grey at the root, eyes clear, utterly
silent — no wingbeat sound, no footfall, no breath you can hear. Not mindless:
it remembers its life and may hesitate at faces it loved. Each one slowly
extends dead air around itself (3m radius: Windworking impossible, hearing
Perception Formidable).*

STR 9, CON 13, SIZ 9, DEX 14, INT 10, POW 14, CHA 4
- Action Points 2 · Damage Mod −1d2 · Initiative +12 · Move 4m walk (CANNOT fly — its wings find no purchase even in living air)
- Skills: Athletics 43, Brawn 38, Endurance 56, Evade 48, Perception 44, Stealth 64 (silence is total: +grade easier in darkness), Willpower 58
- Combat Style — Grasp of the Quiet (unarmed grapple + talons) 45%
- Weapons: Talons 1d4 (S); **Quieting Grip**: on a Grip special effect, the
  victim loses breath — opposed Endurance vs 60 each round held or +1 Fatigue
- Armor: none. Hit locations: avian table.
- Special: immune to Fatigue from exertion and to asphyxiation; takes no
  Stilling penalties. If reduced to 0 HP in head/chest it folds down gently
  and stops, exhaling its whole stolen quiet at once: everyone within 5m makes
  an Endurance roll or is Winded.

## Stillwight
*A predator of the dead zones: a drifting, translucent bell like a glass
jellyfish two meters across, visible mostly as a smear of refracted light.
It feeds by enveloping the head of a breathing creature and drinking the
moving air out of its lungs.*

STR 6, CON 12, SIZ 10, DEX 16, INT 4 (animal), POW 16, CHA —
- Action Points 2 · Damage Mod −1d4 · Initiative +10 · Move 6m drift (any direction; unaffected by still air — it swims in stillness the way fish swim in water)
- Skills: Endurance 54, Evade 52, Perception 58 (senses breath/airflow), Stealth 78 (near-invisible: spotting one unengaged is Formidable Perception)
- Combat Style — Envelop 50% (leaping attack rules; target head location)
- Damage: **Smother** — on a successful Envelop the victim begins asphyxiation
  (Endurance rolls per the SRD asphyxiation rules each round). Struggle free:
  opposed Brawn or Unarmed vs its Envelop.
- Armor: 0, but weapons smaller than M pass through its body harmlessly on
  a normal success — it must be hit with a special effect, a critical, or an
  M+ slashing weapon to take damage. Hit locations: treat all as "Bell"
  (single location, 1-20), HP 8.
- A Stillwight that drinks a full set of lungs (a death) splits into two.

## Sky-Drake
*The mundane apex predator of the windlanes: a quadrupedal raptor-lizard with
a seven-meter wingspan, drake-leather hide, and a shrieking stoop that Vael
couriers learn to hear in their sleep. Territorial; hunts Alar when starving.*

STR 22, CON 15, SIZ 24, DEX 13, INT 5 (animal), POW 10, CHA —
- Action Points 2 · Damage Mod +1d12 · Initiative +9 · Move 6m walk / 14m fly
- Skills: Athletics 65, Brawn 72, Endurance 60, Evade 36, Flight 70, Perception 62, Track 55
- Combat Style — Stoop and Rend (bite/claw/tail) 62%
- Weapons: Bite 1d8 (L), Claw 1d6 (M), Tail-lash 1d6 (M, can Bash)
- Armor: drake-hide 4 AP all locations. Hit locations: winged-quadruped table.
- Tactics: opens with a diving charge (+2 damage mod steps, attack Hard);
  if it loses half its chest HP it disengages and flees.
